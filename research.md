# Ghostline Champions Research Notes

## 1) Pokemon Champions Signals

From official game pages:
- Cross-platform battle title (Nintendo Switch + mobile) centered on trainer battles.
- Core loop includes team construction and training, then battle modes.
- Modes include Ranked Battles, Casual Battles, and Private Battles.
- Battle formats include Single Battles and Double Battles.
- Pokemon HOME integration lets players bring in some partner Pokemon.

Design takeaways used in prototype:
- Champion gauntlet progression (clear sequential opponents).
- Roster strategy (multiple units, swapping mid-fight).
- Turn-based tactical move selection with energy/resource decisions.
